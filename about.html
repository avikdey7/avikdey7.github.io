About

I am a UI/UX Designer based in Helsinki, Finland. I graduated from IIT Kharagpur in 2013. I have over 8 years of experience working on various facets of design in multiple sectors such as ad tech, e-commerce, SaaS, Finance, Travel, Hospitality and Healthcare. During this period, I have taken up a wide range of roles: Design lead, Product manager, UX researcher and Visual designer to name a few. I currently work at Smartly.io, helping build large-scale automation tools for businesses globally.

So, whether you’re a business, a studio, or just want to discuss ideas, feel free to get in touch.
